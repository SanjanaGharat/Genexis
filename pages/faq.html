Getting Started

How do I create an account?

To create your BioXAI account:

  1. Click "Sign Up" on our homepage
  2. Enter your email and create a password
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your health profile questionnaire
  5. Connect your genomic data (optional but recommended)

What data do I need to provide?

For the most accurate epigenetic age predictions, we recommend providing:

  • DNA methylation data (from services like 23andMe or AncestryDNA)
  • Basic health metrics (age, weight, height)
  • Lifestyle information (diet, exercise habits)
  • Wearable device data (Fitbit, Apple Health, etc.)
  • Blood test results (optional for advanced analysis)

How do I connect my wearable device?

To connect your wearable device:

  1. Go to your account settings
  2. Select "Device Connections"
  3. Choose your device from the list
  4. Follow the authorization prompts
  5. Select which data types to share (we recommend all available)
  6. Save your settings

Supported devices include Fitbit, Apple Watch, Garmin, Oura Ring, and most devices that sync with Apple Health or Google Fit.

Understanding Your Results

What does my epigenetic age score mean?

Your epigenetic age score represents your biological age based on DNA methylation patterns, which may differ from your chronological age:

  • Younger than chronological age: Your biological markers are healthier than average for your age group
  • Older than chronological age: Your biological markers show accelerated aging
  • Equal to chronological age: Your biological aging is progressing at the expected rate

A difference of ±3 years is considered normal variation. Larger differences may indicate significant aging acceleration or deceleration.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our epigenetic age predictions have been validated with an accuracy of ±2.1 years in clinical studies. Accuracy depends on:

  • Data quality: Higher quality DNA methylation data provides more accurate results
  • Data completeness: Including wearable and lifestyle data improves accuracy
  • Algorithm version: We continuously improve our models

The intervention recommendations are based on statistical models showing what has worked for similar epigenetic profiles in clinical trials.

How do I interpret the intervention recommendations?

Intervention recommendations are personalized based on your epigenetic profile:

  • Priority level: Indicates how impactful this intervention may be for your profile
  • Evidence strength: Shows the scientific support for this recommendation
  • Expected impact: Estimated epigenetic age reduction over 6-12 months

Recommendations are categorized as:

  • Lifestyle: Diet, exercise, sleep modifications
  • Supplementation: Vitamins, nutraceuticals
  • Therapies: More advanced interventions

Technical Support

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot password?"
  3. Enter the email associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and create a new password
  6. Log in with your new credentials

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

Why isn't my data syncing properly?

If your data isn't syncing properly, try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Disconnect and reconnect the data source
  3. Ensure you've granted all necessary permissions
  4. Update the BioXAI app to the latest version
  5. Restart your device

Common issues include:

  • Outdated device firmware
  • Permission changes after app updates
  • Service outages with third-party providers

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our support team through:

  • In-app messaging: Go to Settings > Help > Contact Support
  • Email: support@bioxai.com (response within 24 hours)
  • Live chat: Available weekdays 9AM-5PM EST

For faster assistance, please include:

  • Your account email
  • Description of the issue
  • Screenshots if applicable
  • Device and OS information
